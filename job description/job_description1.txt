Job Title: Data Analyst
We are looking for a Data Analyst to collect, process, and analyze data to help support business decisions. The candidate should be able to interpret data trends and provide actionable insights.

Key Responsibilities:
Analyze large datasets using statistical techniques
Create reports and dashboards using tools like Excel, SQL, or Power BI
Identify trends and patterns in data
Work with teams to understand business requirements

Required Skills:
Proficiency in SQL and Excel
Basic knowledge of Python or R
Experience with data visualization tools (Tableau/Power BI)
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills

Preferred Qualifications:
Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Statistics, or related field
Internship or project experience in data analysis
